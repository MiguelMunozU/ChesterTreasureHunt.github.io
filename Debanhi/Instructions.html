Welcome to Chester's Treasure Hunt adventure!

Follow these instructions to embark on your journey to find the hidden treasure:

  1. 1. Map Exploration

    • Explore the Map: Begin by exploring the map provided to you. Identify marked locations where clues and riddles are hidden.

  2. 2. Interact with Special Items

    • Click on Special Items: Navigate to each location on the map and click on its respective special item. These items may look familiar to you! They will trigger the appearance of riddles.

  3. 3. Solving Riddles

    • Read the Riddles: Once you click on a special item, a riddle will appear on your screen. Read it carefully; it holds clues to uncovering the treasure's location.
    • Answer the Riddle: Use your wit and knowledge to answer the riddle correctly! Type your answer into the provided text box or submit your guess.

  4. 4. Discovering Clues

    • Reveal Clues: Upon answering the riddle correctly, you will receive a clue that hints at the next location or a piece of the puzzle leading to the treasure.

  5. 5. Finding the Treasure

    • Follow the Clues: Continue visiting locations, interacting with special items, and solving riddles to collect all the clues.
    • Uncover the Treasure: Piece together all the clues to uncover the final location where the treasure is hidden. It could be a specific place, a secret spot on the map, or a final riddle leading directly to the treasure.

  6. 6. Celebrate Your Success

    • Claim Your Reward: Once you find the treasure, celebrate your success! You've completed Chester's Treasure Hunt and found the hidden treasure.
